EXPERIENCE US

We’re Your Partner in Your Success

SoftWorld International founded in 1999, headquartered in Karachi, Pakistan, to help companies with their IT solutions. Our initial focus was on providing companies with complete IT solutions. In response to the needs of the customer and to fully cover the Scope of IT services.

SoftWorld International offers a wide range of information technology solutions designed to improve market efficiency. From expert installation, outsourcing and implementation to hosted solutions.

SoftWorld International works closely with leading technology partners to develop value-added Solutions for customers.

SoftWorld is an authorized partner/reseller of Microsoft, IBM, Symantec, Acronis, VMware, Cisco, Adobe, Blackbox, Intel, GFI, Solarwinds, Ipswitch, Corel, Intel, McAfee, Autodesk and CA products in Pakistan.

Today, based in Pakistan, we deliver the most comprehensive suite of managed IT services to small and medium-sized enterprises across Asia and the Middle East.
