Corel

Corel Corporation from the abbreviation ("Cowpland Research Laboratory") is a computer software company headquartered in Ottawa, Ontario, that specializes in graphics processing. It is known for producing software titles such as CorelDRAW, Paint Shop Pro and later acquired major competitors such as Ulead Systems and WordPerfect.

Corel's products include VideoStudio Pro, Paint Shop Pro Photo, Painter, WinDVD, DVD XPack, CorelDRAW Graphics Suite, Photo & Video Bundle, WordPerfect Office, Painter and Wacom Bundle, PhotoImpact.

A number of Corel products are used in the creation of clients' web pages including CorelDRAW X5 and the WebMaster suite of applications.
